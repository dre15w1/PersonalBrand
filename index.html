01 · Market Data & Broker
Charles Schwab Trader API integration with OAuth2 and auto-refresh. Real-time WebSocket streaming, Level 2 book depth multiplexed on a single account socket, full option chain with live Greeks, multi-interval OHLCV, and a 2.76M-row local cache with rate-limit governor and yfinance failover.
OAuth2 WebSocket Level 2 Live Greeks
02 · Strategy Engine
19 hot-swappable strategy modules behind a common scan interface — market structure (BOS, CHoCH, fractal pivots), smart-money concepts (FVGs, supply/demand, liquidity sweeps, order blocks), volume profile, order flow, and classical technicals. Every signal emits a human-readable explanation of why it fired.
BOS / CHoCH Fair Value Gaps Order Flow MTF Confluence
03 · Options Pricing & Selection
Black-Scholes pricing with correct time-decay across the holding period. Delta-targeted strike selection with dynamic expiry rules (0DTE / 1DTE / weekly). Contract quality gates on spread, open interest, volume, and stale Greeks — plus IV-vs-realized comparison for premium richness.
Black-Scholes Delta Targeting 0DTE / 1DTE
04 · Risk Management
Position sizing derived from underlying invalidation levels, not premium available. Structural stops that exit on the stock breaking structure — not on premium moves caused by spread or volatility. Circuit breakers, daily loss limits, consecutive-loss cooldowns, earnings gating, and an account-level kill switch.
Circuit Breakers Structural Stops Kill Switch
05 · ML Confirmation
Per-symbol adaptive models with nightly automated retraining. Regime-conditioned variants (gamma-positive, gamma-negative, near-max-pain). AI confirmation layer that can veto or downsize signals below a confidence threshold. Feature extraction from order book state, volume profile, and market structure.
scikit-learn Nightly Retraining Regime Models
06 · Research Harness
Event-driven backtester with explicit execution cost modeling — spread, decay, commissions. Gap-aware fills. Monte Carlo simulation and bootstrap confidence intervals on profit factor. Walk-forward validation. Parameter optimizers with performance attribution by strategy, symbol, hour, and regime.
Monte Carlo Walk-Forward Bootstrap CIs
