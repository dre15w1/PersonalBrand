Andre Walker is an enterprise IT leader who founded and sold a fintech marketplace, leads applications development for a national energy company, and — when the markets close — drives a McLaren.

The day job is real: at Stryten Energy, he manages a cross-functional team of seven across enterprise application development, SaaS platforms, and analytics & automation. The CV behind it is deeper: nine years at UPS Capital leading mission-critical financial reconciliation platforms, three more at Home Depot consolidating omnichannel fulfillment, and a Georgia Pacific tour that generated $4M+ in annual operational savings.

Today the studio behind it all, Walkers Software Development LLC, builds custom software for clients who want technology done right.
